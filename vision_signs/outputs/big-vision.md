# The Sign

> ## Trails End: A Vision in Action
>
> *Where Camping, Farming, and Community Come Together*
>
> This property represents a new way of thinking about land use - what we call "Ecology 3.0." Instead of separating different activities into isolated zones, we layer them together to create a thriving, sustainable community.
>

# The Design

| **Sign Type** | Vision (Comprehensive) |
| **Location** | Pump House - Central location for comprehensive overview |

## Sign Goals

- **Comprehensive overview** - Tie together all aspects of the property
- **Vision explanation** - Explain the core philosophy and values
- **Artifact inventory** - List the key elements visitors will encounter
- **Directional guidance** - Point visitors to other signs and areas
- **Community building** - Emphasize the shared vision and values
- **Inspiration** - Motivate visitors to explore and learn

## Design Notes

- Large, prominent display that serves as the central information hub
- Include map showing all key areas and sign locations
- Use multiple panels or sections to organize information
- Emphasize the integration and layering concepts
- Include contact information for tours or questions
