# The Design

| **Sign Type** | 3-30-3 (3-30 second to 3 minute reading) |
| **Location** | Welcome Board - Main entrance area |

## Sign Goals

- **Farm introduction** - Welcome visitors to the agricultural aspect
- **Vision explanation** - Introduce the "Ecology 3.0" concept
- **Experience preview** - Set expectations for what they'll discover
- **Trail promotion** - Encourage Orchard Trail exploration
- **Produce connection** - Direct to The Genny for fresh food
- **Community building** - Emphasize the shared vision

## Design Notes

- Position as the "agricultural welcome" to complement campground welcome
- Include small map showing key farm areas
- Emphasize the integration with camping experience
- Use warm, inviting tone that matches the community feel
