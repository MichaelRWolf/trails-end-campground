## Sign Goals

- **Orchard identification** - Name and explain this specific growing area
- **Educational content** - Teach about fruit growing and orchard management
- **Local connection** - Include trivia about fruit growing in this specific locale
- **Produce promotion** - Direct visitors to The Genny for fresh fruit
- **Vision alignment** - Connect to the broader "eat local" story

## Design Notes

- Include seasonal information about harvest times
- Add local growing trivia specific to fruit production in this region
- Emphasize the unique characteristics of this orchard area
