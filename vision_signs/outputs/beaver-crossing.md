# Beaver Crossing Sign

---

## 🦫 Beaver Crossing 🦫

**We are living in harmony with nature**

*[Directional arrows pointing both ways]*

---

## 👤 Human Crossing 👤

**We are living in harmony with nature**

*[Directional arrows pointing both ways]*

---

## Sign Type

3-x-x (3 second reading)

## Location

Orchard Trail - where beaver trails cross human trails

## Sign Goals

- **Playful engagement** - Catch attention with cute beaver imagery
- **Quick message** - Reinforce harmony with nature concept
- **Trail guidance** - Help visitors understand they're sharing space with wildlife
- **Brand personality** - Show the fun, approachable side of the campground

## Design Notes

- **Two-sided sign** - "Beaver Crossing" in one direction, "Human Crossing" at right angles
- Use directional arrows pointing both ways from each side
- Include simple beaver and human silhouettes/emojis
- Keep text minimal and friendly
- Emphasize the "harmony" concept from recreational_areas.md
- **Playful approach** - Make it cute and engaging, not just informational
- **Right-angle placement** - Sign should be positioned so both messages are visible from their respective trail directions
