# The Sign

> ## Welcome to Trails End Campground
>
> *More Than Just a Place to Sleep*
>
> Welcome to a unique camping experience where primitive camping meets working agriculture and community business. Here, you'll sleep next to the fields that grow your food, explore trails that connect to a regional network, and discover a new way of thinking about how land can serve multiple purposes.
>
> **What makes us different:**
>
> - Campsites adjacent to working farm fields
> - Integration with Soil & Soul Farm operations
> - Connection to Copper Harbor trail system
> - Community businesses that support local economy
> - A vision of sustainable, multi-use land management
>
> **Notice our ethic of trust** - no guards, no heavy check-in procedures. We believe in community, respect, and shared stewardship of this special place.
>
> **Explore the Orchard Trail** to discover our story → (with arrow)

# The Design

| **Sign Type** | 3-30-3 (3-30 second to 3 minute reading) |
| **Location** | Welcome Board - Main entrance area |

## Sign Goals

- **Campground introduction** - Welcome visitors to the camping experience
- **Unique positioning** - Explain what makes this different from typical campgrounds
- **Trust emphasis** - Highlight the "ethic of trust" mentioned in recreational_areas.md
- **Integration story** - Show how camping connects to farm and business
- **Trail promotion** - Encourage Orchard Trail exploration
- **Community values** - Emphasize shared stewardship and respect

## Design Notes

- Complement the Soil & Soul Farm welcome sign
- Emphasize the "more than just camping" concept
- Include small map showing campground layout
- Use welcoming but informative tone
