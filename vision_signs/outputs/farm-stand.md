# Farm Stand Sign

---

## Farm Stand

*More Than a Cooler Full of Veggies*

This is more than a farm stand. More than a cooler full of veggies. It is an expression of our love for the land. Veggies grown less than 2 miles from here by people who care a lot about health, soil, soul, connections (to land and people and ourselves).

**Please visit us.** Where this food is grown. View the beautiful fields in a park-like environment. Take a walk. See the views. Relax in nature and feel what it feels like to be connected to your food, the people who grew the food, and the land the food was grown on.

**Start your farm experience** - Follow the Orchard Trail to see where your food grows → (with arrow)

---

## Sign Type

3-30-3 (3-30 second to 3 minute reading)

## Location

The Genny (Gaslight General Store) - Farm stand area

## Sign Goals

- **Transform perception** - Change from "POS vending machine" to meaningful experience
- **Sell the vision** - Emphasize the story and values behind the produce
- **Emotional connection** - Create connection to land, people, and food
- **Farm promotion** - Encourage visitors to explore the actual growing areas
- **Community building** - Emphasize the people and relationships behind the food
- **Experience invitation** - Invite visitors to take the Orchard Trail tour

## Design Notes

- Position prominently at The Genny farm stand area
- Use warm, inviting tone that matches the emotional content
- Emphasize the "less than 2 miles" local connection
- Include directional arrow to Orchard Trail
- Consider including photos of the fields or farmers
- Make it clear this is about more than just buying vegetables
