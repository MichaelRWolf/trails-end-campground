# Farm Stand Sign

---

## Farm Stand @ The Genny

*More Than a Cooler Full of Veggies*

This 24/7 self-service farm stand is more than a refrigerator full of vegetables. It's an expression of our love for the land. Every item was grown less than 2 miles from here by people who care deeply about health, soil, soul, and connections (to land, to people, to ourselves).

**This sign transforms our presentation.** What you see now - a storage refrigerator with scribbled prices - doesn't reflect the care we put into growing this food. This sign tells the real story of what you're buying and invites you to experience where it comes from.

**Please visit us.** Where this food is grown. View the beautiful fields in a park-like environment. Take a walk. See the views. Relax in nature and feel what it feels like to be connected to your food, the people who grew it, and the land it was grown on.

**Start your farm experience** - Follow the Orchard Trail to see where your food grows → (with arrow)

---

## Sign Type

3-30-3 (3-30 second to 3 minute reading)

## Location

The Genny's porch - 24/7 self-service farm stand (concession space provided by The Genny)

## Sign Goals

- **Transform presentation** - Elevate from "storage refrigerator with scribbled prices" to meaningful experience
- **Acknowledge reality** - Be honest about current presentation while telling deeper story
- **Sell the vision** - Emphasize the story and values behind the produce
- **Emotional connection** - Create connection to land, people, and food
- **Farm promotion** - Encourage visitors to explore the actual growing areas (less than 2 miles)
- **Community building** - Emphasize the people and relationships behind the food
- **Experience invitation** - Invite visitors to take the Orchard Trail tour
- **24/7 availability** - Highlight self-service convenience

## Design Notes

- Position prominently on/near the farm stand refrigerator on The Genny's porch
- Use warm, inviting tone that matches the emotional content
- Acknowledge current presentation reality ("storage refrigerator with scribbled prices")
- Frame the sign itself as part of the transformation/improvement
- Emphasize the "less than 2 miles" ultra-local connection
- Highlight 24/7 self-service availability and iPad POS system
- Include directional arrow to Orchard Trail
- Consider including photos of the fields or farmers
- Make it clear this is about more than just buying vegetables - it's about connection
- This sign helps elevate curb appeal and tells the story the current presentation doesn't
